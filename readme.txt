Adds waitlist functionality to Tutor LMS courses, enabling admins to notify waitlisted students when a spot becomes available.

== Description ==

**Waitlist For Tutor LMS** is the must-have addon for managing **fully booked** courses in Tutor LMS. With this powerful tool, students can join a **waitlist** when a course is full and get instant **email notifications** when spots become available.

👥 Stop losing students due to full courses—turn them into future enrollments with Waitlist For Tutor LMS!

🔹 **Key Features:**  
✅ **"Join Waitlist"** – Automatically appears on fully booked Tutor LMS courses
📧 **Email Notifications** – Instantly notifies students when a spot becomes available
📊 **Admin Dashboard** – Manage Waitlists and Enrolled Users (From Waitlist)
📢 **Bulk "Notify All"** – One-click email alert to all waitlisted students
🎯 **Individual Actions** – Notify or remove students with a single click
📂 **Export to CSV** – Download waitlist data for easy record-keeping
🟠 **Visual Status Indicators** – 🟠 "Waiting" | ✅ "Notified" for quick tracking
✨ **Modern UI** – Clean, user-friendly interface for efficient management
🔢 **Waitlist Counter** – Displays the total waitlist count in the admin header
🔄 **Seamless Tutor LMS Integration** – Automatically detects enrolled users

== 🎯 How It Works ==  

📌 **For Students:**  
1️⃣ If a course is full, a "Join Waitlist" button appears.  
2️⃣ Students enter their email and join the waitlist.  
3️⃣ They receive an **email notification** when a seat opens.  

📌 **For Administrators:**  
1️⃣ Access the **Waitlist Dashboard** in Tutor LMS.  
2️⃣ View and manage waitlist entries.  
3️⃣ Notify students individually or in bulk.  
4️⃣ Track students who enrolled from the waitlist.  
5️⃣ Export waitlist data for records.  

💡 **This plugin requires [Tutor LMS](https://wordpress.org/plugins/tutor/) to function.**

== Installation == 

1️⃣ Upload the plugin folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`  
2️⃣ Activate it via the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress  
3️⃣ Ensure **Tutor LMS** is installed and active  
4️⃣ The waitlist feature will now automatically integrate with fully booked courses  

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this plugin require Tutor LMS? =
Yes, it’s an addon designed specifically for Tutor LMS and requires it to function.

= Can administrators notify users multiple times? =
Yes, admins can notify waitlisted students repeatedly. The status updates to "Notified," but entries remain manageable in the dashboard.

= How are enrolled users identified? =
The plugin matches waitlist emails with Tutor LMS enrollment data, moving enrolled users to a separate "Enrolled Users (From Waitlist)" section.

= Is there a limit to the waitlist size? =
No, there’s no inherent limit to the number of students who can join a waitlist.

= Where do I manage the waitlist? =
Under the Tutor LMS menu in the WordPress admin area, look for "Waitlists" to access the management dashboard.
